Tetris Clone

solo project

Development: May 2021 - August 2021

Language: C++

Engine: Custom, with SFML for low-level graphics support

After completing my freshman year at DigiPen, I wanted to keep my skills sharp and practice what I had been learning, especially custom engine development. So, I spent half of the summer creating my own component- based engine, with all the systems and GameObject components that I or someone else would need to make a game of their own. Then when I was happy with my progress, I tested this engine by creating "temtris" and everything went smoothly! This was a great exercise to test my knowledge and competence as a game developer, and now I have a solid engine that I can use to easily create other projects and expand upon in later years as I learn even more.
